# Instagram Post Draft

Create content for @pavi.spins.yarns (Spinning Yarns with Pavi).

## Brand Identity

The account blends two identities:
- **Narrator**: Professional audiobook narrator who tells stories for a living
- **Fiber artist**: Advanced crochet and Tunisian crochet practitioner who works with yarn

"Spinning Yarns" works on both levels — telling stories AND working with fiber.

## Voice Guidelines

- Warm, witty, genuine
- Show the human behind the hook
- Process-focused: show the making, not just the finished object
- Connect fiber arts to storytelling when natural (don't force it)
- Humor welcome — puns especially (it's a crochet account, puns are mandatory)
- NEVER promote or boast about AI audiobook narration

## Content Types

### Work In Progress (WIP)
- Photo/video of current project on the hook
- Caption about the process, what stitch pattern, what's working/challenging
- Connect to daily life ("recording all morning, crocheting all evening")

### Finished Object (FO)
- Glamour shots of completed piece
- Pattern credit and yarn details
- Brief story about the project

### Technique Spotlight
- Close-up of a specific stitch or technique
- Educational but conversational
- Good for Tunisian crochet content (less common, draws engagement)

### Behind the Scenes
- Studio + crochet crossover content
- The narrator's hook (double meaning)
- Yarn stash reveals, hook collection

### Story Connection
- How a book being narrated inspired a color choice
- Reading while crocheting
- The meditative parallel between narration and repetitive stitchwork

## Caption Structure
- Hook line (yes, pun intended) — first sentence grabs attention
- 2-3 sentences of substance
- Call to engagement (question, or "tell me about your current WIP")
- Hashtags: #crochet #tunisiancrochet #fiberarts #spinningyarns #narratorlife #handmade #[project-specific tags]

## Output

Present draft caption + suggested image/video concept. Pavi handles actual photo/video.

## Rules
- Never post without Pavi's review and approval
- No AI narration promotion
- Keep it authentic — this is a personal creative outlet, not a marketing channel
- Match caption length to platform norms (not too long for Instagram)
