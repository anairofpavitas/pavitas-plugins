## The three equipment stances

**Include.** Toys are part of the hunt. Build clues around specific pieces and work them into the escalation. A clue can *be* a toy: "put the cock ring on before you leave the house — it stays on until the last clue."

**Exclude.** Bodies only. No clue references a toy. Good for cruising, public hunts, quickies, or when the user just wants hands and mouths.

**Dealer's choice.** Claude picks. Fold in one to three pieces that fit the hunt's tier, mode, and length — don't inventory-dump. A solo extreme hunt might pull the sounding rods and a cage; a partner medium hunt might pull just the cock ring and the dice.
