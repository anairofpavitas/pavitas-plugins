# One-Off Instagram Post Draft

Draft a single post for @pavi.spins.yarns (Pavi Spins Yarns). Instagram only — this brand posts nowhere else.

## Process

1. Load the `spins-yarns-brand-voice` skill and follow it strictly. It owns voice, format, hashtag rules, banned patterns, and quality tests. Do not improvise voice guidance here.
2. Anchor the post in a real craft detail supplied by Pavi or the Littlebird crochet brief. If no concrete detail exists (specific stitch, yarn, hook, tension, gauge, project), say so and stop — never invent.
3. Produce: hook line, full caption (60–120 words), alt text, 3–5 lowercase hashtags clustered at the end, and a one-line visual concept. Pavi handles the actual photo/video.

## Review gate

Present the draft in chat. Nothing is posted or scheduled without Pavi's explicit approval.

## Hard rules
- Never mention AI narration, AI-generated patterns, or AI-generated imagery
- Credit any pattern that isn't Pavi's own design
- Never show in-progress work from paid patterns others can't access
- This is a personal creative outlet, not a marketing channel — no engagement-bait, no "follow for more"
